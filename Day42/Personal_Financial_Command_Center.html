Tips, Checklist & Resources

Quick money literacy for students — check items off as you build the habit.

Money habits checklist
    Ask your AI assistant

    Copy a prompt into Claude to go deeper on your finances:

    • 💬 "Review my last 10 expenses and suggest one habit to change."
    • 💬 "Help me build a 3-month plan to reach my savings goal faster."
    • 💬 "Explain compound interest like I'm saving for a laptop."
    • 💬 "What's a realistic student budget split for my allowance?"

    🏺 Pay yourself first

    Move a fixed amount to savings the moment allowance lands — before it can be spent.

    📊 The 50/30/20 idea

    Roughly 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings — adjust the split to fit a student budget.

    🔁 Audit subscriptions monthly

    Small recurring charges are the easiest money to win back.

Here are the key learnings from the Decision Strategist:

🎯 Define the real decision before trying to solve it.

❓ Ask one focused question at a time to uncover the root issue.

🧠 Separate facts, assumptions, emotions, and uncertainties.

⚖️ Evaluate options objectively instead of choosing based on bias.

📊 Compare trade-offs, risks, benefits, and long-term impact.

🎯 Align decisions with your goals, values, and priorities.

🔍 Challenge hidden assumptions that may influence your thinking.

💡 Consider alternative perspectives before reaching a conclusion.

📈 Make decisions using structured reasoning rather than impulse.

✅ End with a clear, well-supported decision and actionable next steps.

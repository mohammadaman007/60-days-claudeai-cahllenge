📖 What is Context Engineering?
Context Engineering is the practice of providing AI with the right information, background, constraints, and goals so it can generate more accurate, relevant, and useful responses.
Think of it this way:
Prompt Engineering = Asking the question
Context Engineering = Providing the information AI needs to answer well
The better the context, the better the output.

Key Concepts of Context Engineering
1. Current Situation
Tell AI who you are.
Examples:
Student
Data Analyst
Freelancer
Entrepreneur
Why it matters: AI tailors recommendations based on your role.

What i Learned
After understanding Context Engineering, you should know:
Context is often more important than the prompt itself.
AI performs better when given background information.
Clear goals produce more focused outputs.
Constraints help AI generate realistic solutions.
Personalization comes from context, not just instructions.
Better context reduces the need for multiple follow-up prompts.
Context Engineering is a core skill for effective AI use.

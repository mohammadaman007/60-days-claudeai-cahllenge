What is this?

A quick orientation, available any time.

Personal AI Playbook is a personal toolkit of reusable prompt templates ("workflows") built specifically for how you study — practice questions, self-testing, and structured notes in STEM subjects, using Claude.

Instead of hunting for a good prompt every time, you keep a library of tested, editable templates, and two builders to make new ones:

  • My Workflows — your saved, ready-to-use prompt templates, grouped by what they help you do.
  • Building Blocks — the underlying pieces (role, objective, format, etc.) that any prompt is made of.
  • Prompt Builder — assemble a new one-shot prompt from blocks, with a live preview.
  • Loop Builder — turn a prompt into a self-checking, repeating routine (e.g. "keep quizzing me until I get 8/10 right").

Everything is saved in your browser's local storage — nothing leaves this file. Use Export to back up or move your data.

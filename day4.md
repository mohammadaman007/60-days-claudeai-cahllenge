
What is Chain of Thought Prompting?
Chain of Thought (CoT) Prompting is a technique where you instruct an AI to reason through a problem step by step before giving the final answer.
Instead of jumping directly to the answer, the AI breaks the problem into smaller logical steps.
Without Chain of Thought
Prompt:
What is the profit if revenue is ₹50,000 and expenses are ₹35,000?
Response:
₹15,000
Correct, but no explanation.
With Chain of Thought
Prompt:
Think step by step and calculate the profit if revenue is ₹50,000 and expenses are ₹35,000.
Response:
Revenue = ₹50,000
Expenses = ₹35,000
Profit = Revenue − Expenses
Profit = ₹15,000
Final Answer: ₹15,000
Why Chain of Thought Matters
When tasks become complex, AI can make mistakes if it jumps directly to conclusions.
CoT helps AI:
✅ Break problems into smaller parts
✅ Reduce reasoning errors
✅ Improve accuracy
✅ Handle complex tasks better
✅ Explain decisions clearly
How Chain of Thought Works
Instead of:
Input → Answer
It becomes:
Input → Reasoning Steps → Answer
This mirrors how humans solve difficult problems.

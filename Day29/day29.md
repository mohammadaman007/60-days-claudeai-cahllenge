🚚 Key Learnings from Building “Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab.

1. Supply chains are not only about moving products — they are about managing uncertainty and making decisions under pressure.
2. Every crisis creates trade-offs between cost, speed, inventory, and customer satisfaction.
3. Strong supplier relationships can become a competitive advantage during disruption.
4. Leadership decisions directly influence business resilience and recovery outcomes.
5. AI creates more value when it supports forecasting, risk monitoring, and decision-making.
6. Customer trust is built through consistent delivery and transparent actions.
7. Long-term resilience often matters more than short-term optimization.
8. Data-driven decisions improve operational performance and business continuity.

This project helped me understand how strategy, operations, leadership, and technology connect in real-world supply chain management.

Full action plan:

1

Audit app permissions immediately

Go to phone settings and revoke camera, microphone, location, and contacts from any app that doesn't strictly need them — especially TikTok, Snapchat, and PUBG Mobile.

2

Turn off ad personalisation on Google

Visit myaccount.google.com > Data & Privacy > Ad Settings. This limits cross-service profiling across your 4 Google-owned services.

3

Review Meta's off-Facebook activity

Meta tracks you across third-party websites. Go to Instagram Settings > Security > Off-Facebook Activity to clear and limit this.

4

Enable TikTok's privacy controls (or consider deleting)

TikTok collects significant behavioural data. If you keep it, limit personalisation in Settings > Privacy. Consider its high data exposure risk.

5

Use strong unique passwords and 2FA

With 15 services, a single breached credential is dangerous. Use a password manager and enable 2FA on every account, especially Google, WhatsApp, and Amazon.

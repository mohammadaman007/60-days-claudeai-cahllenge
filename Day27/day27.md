Key Learnings — Prior Authorization Story Simulator

About the Project

Developed a Prior Authorization Story Simulator with support from Claude to explore and visualize healthcare authorization workflows through an interactive, story-based educational experience.

This project focused on simplifying a complex healthcare process into an accessible and engaging interface while strengthening both technical and product-thinking skills.

What I Learned

🏥 Understanding Healthcare Workflows

Learned how Prior Authorization works as a structured review process before treatment approval and how workflow decisions influence patient experiences.

👥 Multi-Stakeholder System Thinking

Explored the responsibilities and interactions across:

- Patients
- Providers
- Payers

and how coordination affects outcomes.

📄 Role of Documentation & Decision Flow

Understood how elements such as:

- Clinical documentation
- Diagnosis alignment
- Treatment history
- Appeals and approvals

contribute to workflow progression.

💡 Product Thinking & Problem Solving

Focused on transforming a complex operational process into a clear, beginner-friendly educational product.

🤖 AI-Assisted Development Experience

Used Claude as a development support tool for:

- Structuring application logic
- Refining workflow flow design
- Organizing UI interactions
- Accelerating prototyping and iteration

This experience showed me that AI can support development speed, while understanding the problem and making design decisions remains essential.

🎨 User Experience & Educational Design

Worked on creating:

- Story-driven interactions

Reflection

This project helped me connect software development, product thinking, and real-world healthcare processes while exploring how AI tools like Claude can support learning and building.

Key Learnings from This Topic (Decision Strategist Prompt)

🎯 Learn to make decisions using a structured framework instead of emotions alone.

❓ Break complex decisions into clear questions before jumping to conclusions.

⚖️ Compare options objectively by evaluating trade-offs, risks, and long-term impact.

🧠 Identify hidden assumptions and cognitive biases that influence your thinking.

📊 Use a decision matrix to score options based on meaningful criteria.

💥 Apply a premortem to anticipate why a decision could fail before committing.

🧪 Test uncertain choices with small experiments instead of making irreversible commitments.

📅 Follow a time-bound decision process to avoid analysis paralysis.

🔄 Consider reversibility—some decisions are easier to change than others.

📈 Prioritize long-term goals and values over short-term emotions.

💬 Gather relevant information before analyzing or choosing.

🚀 Present insights visually through an interactive dashboard for faster understanding.
